# Code of Conduct

FastADB uses the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept corrections.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Give and receive technical feedback with patience.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, insults, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Repeatedly derailing project discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject PRs, or block participants whose behavior is harmful to the project community.

Instances of unacceptable behavior can be reported to the maintainers through GitHub. Reports will be reviewed as privately and fairly as possible.
